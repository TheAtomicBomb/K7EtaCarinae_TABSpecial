K7 Eta Carinae TAB Special is a SFZ soundfont created by TheAtomicBomb based on the remixed and modulated K7 Eta Carinae sampled by IMT11D. Its soft, warm and slightly tough sound is suitable for playing lyrical, relaxed, distant and clear melodic black scores.
With its design of separating wet and dry samples, the soundfont offers the new ability to change the reverb effect by adjusting internal parameters. You can switch and create .sfz presets freely, and enjoy the high quality reverb that comes from being in a concert hall in post-production.

Caution:	

*Please turn off the reverb that comes with MIDI synthesizer to get the purest reverb of this soundfont (e.g. check the "Disable MIDI effects rendering" option of coolsoft).

*The actual polyphony count of all presets except Dry and Reverb is twice that of the normal soundfont, so please adjust the polyphony count according to your computer's performance to avoid missing sounds; when converting audio with this library, you can turn up the polyphony count for the best listening effect.
